* Users: Executive, Bid Manager, Solution Lead, Commercial and Finance, Legal, Sourcing and Vendor, Analyst, Admin.
* Primary value: SLA and escalation to avoid late submissions. Single source for RFP docs, clarifications, compliance matrix, pricing and approvals. Automated award ingestion to track winners, award values, and business activity codes. Fast executive insight through dashboards.
* Key jobs:

  * Create and move opportunities across Kanban and timeline.
  * Build compliance matrices from RFP PDFs with verbatim requirement text and our response.
  * Manage clarifications with numbering, exports, and linked responses.
  * Manage BoQ items, vendor quotes, price guardrails, and approvals.
  * Generate and freeze submission packs.
  * Record outcomes with reason codes and lessons learned.
* Non-goals: Full CRM, contract management after award, and vendor payment workflows.
